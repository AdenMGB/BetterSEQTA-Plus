What is SHA-256?

Think of SHA-256 as a magical fingerprint machine for digital data:

  • Takes any digital input (like a message or file)
  • Always produces a unique 256-bit (64 character) fingerprint
  • Even a tiny change creates a completely different fingerprint!

Why SHA-256 is Strong üí™

  • Collision Resistance: Like finding two people with identical fingerprints
  • One-way Function: Can't reconstruct original data from hash
  • Avalanche Effect: Tiny changes cause completely different outputs
